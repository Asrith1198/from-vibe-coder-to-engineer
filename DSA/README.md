# 🧠 Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

> "Bad programmers worry about the code. Good programmers worry about data structures and their relationships." — Linus Torvalds

Mastering DSA is the single most important step to becoming a resilient engineer capable of building high-performance systems and cracking premium tech jobs.

## 🏆 Goals & DSA Roadmap

- [ ] **Arrays & Strings** (Fundamentals, sliding window, two pointers)
- [ ] **Linked Lists** (Singly, Doubly, Circular, Floyd's Cycle detection)
- [ ] **Stacks & Queues** (Implementation, Monotonic stack, priority queues)
- [ ] **Trees & Graphs** (Binary Trees, BST, DFS, BFS, Djikstra's, Graph traversals)
- [ ] **Recursion & Backtracking** (N-Queens, Sudoku solver, subset generation)
- [ ] **Dynamic Programming** (Memoization, Tabulation, Knapsack, LIS)
